the direction so that the remaining edges then give some texture and shape

From: splendidbuccaneer | Date: 2004-11-16 20:28:16 UTC
I really like this stamp, too, and since I'm sitting here with it on
my desk, I know how tiny it is.. about 1" by 1". Which just amazes
me that you could get those tiny details in there!! My carving
skills are no way better than your's...you'll see that it's a lot
easier to carve details, etc., when you're carving in a big old hunk
of the good stuff! I do have a number 1 nib that I heated up and
squished to make a fine detail nib... works great.

I'm probably going to plant your little kiwi somewhere around Cherry
Creek Reservoir in Denver. It's a big park area with lots of trees
and trails and good spots.. but also fairly close to everyone and
easy to get to. Woohoo! There are some letterboxers champing at
the bit to find him!

Esmerelda
