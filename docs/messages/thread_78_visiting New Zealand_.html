From: Chaucey McLachlan | Date: 2005-01-22 21:47:55 UTC
Hello!

That is really exciting news. I hope that we'll be able to meet up and have
an "exchange". I live in Wellington.

Do you know yet which cities or areas you'll be travelling through? If you
are following the main highway (state highway 1) which runs the length of
the country, you will pass within 25km's of (currently) 23 "published"
boxes. There are currently a further 11 boxes slightly further afield.
Psychokiwi helped the Atlasquest site to develop a trip planner which will
show you this information.

You've got a whole month so you should be able to see lots! There aren't
many boxes in the bottom half of the South island yet, which is a shame
because that is beautiful country but I'm sure you'd still enjoy checking it
out. What sort of non-letterboxing interests do you have? Perhaps that'd
give us an idea of what advice would help.

The only LOTR box that I'm aware of is actually one of my own. It is called
Isengard Gardens and is located at the film location for filming scenes of
Isengard Gardens, approx half and hours drive from central Wellington. The
stamp is not particulary artisitic but is themed appropriately and I made it
myself.
There are quite a few LOTR themed geocaches if you are into geocaching too.

Let us know what other sort of info you need and I'm sure we'll all be keen
to help. Everyone on this forum is really friendly.

Chaucey
