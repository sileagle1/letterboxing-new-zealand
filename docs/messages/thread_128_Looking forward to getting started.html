Glad to hear from someone keen to get started on letterboxing. Where

AtlasQuest, which contains the clues to most NZ letterboxes has a 'pet
friendly' search criteria which means it's located in an area that
allows pets to roam, usually with a leash requirement. We've always
marked our boxes with this criteria if they're suitable for dogs,and
hopefully most others have as well.

We've recently got a puppy who we'll start to take on some of our
letterboxing adventures, so we'll also be looking out for dog friendly
boxes from now on.
