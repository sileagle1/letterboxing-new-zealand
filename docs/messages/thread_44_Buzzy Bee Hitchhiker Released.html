Buzzy Bee set out on a mission to fly all around New Zealand! He started off his adventures in a South Island letterbox and is itching to start flying again. If you find Buzzy Bee, stamp both your stamp and the host-box stamp into his logbook and vice-versa, then move Buzzy Bee to a different letterbox - preferably one he hasn't visited before.
