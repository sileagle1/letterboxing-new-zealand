The clues seemed to work just fine - I actually scribbled down notes rather
than print out the page (I was too lazy to go upstairs and turn the printer
on) so any uncertainties we had were probably due to my cryptic scribbles.
My only suggestion would be to also use ziplock-style plastic bags to
protect the logbook (I usually carry spares, but didn't have one on me).
Your box was fine - perfectly dry inside - but I don't know longterm how
waterproof those boxes are so I like to double or even triple protect
against moisture. Bags also protect against the ink pad leaking. We have
found too many sodden, wet, and even horribly mouldy letterboxes - and NZ is
much wetter than Connecticut.

Cheers,

Judi
