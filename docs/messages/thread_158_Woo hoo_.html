From: Wisconsin Hiker | Date: 2009-11-20 05:08:15 UTC
I was very happy to get two emails this month that 2 boxes we planted two years ago have finally had visitors!

Almost exactly 2 years ago we spent 3 weeks vacationing in New Zealand. While we were there we planted 2 boxes on the South Island and 3 on the North Island. All had a Maori theme. The NI boxes were found after 1 year and now the SI boxes have been found after 2 years. It was nice to hear that they are still in good shape. I think each box has only had one visitor - 55 Steps for 4 of the boxes and Norfolk Nomads (from USA) for 1 box.
