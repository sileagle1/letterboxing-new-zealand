We found the Happy Humpback!

From: Rebecca and Derek | Date: 2004-09-13 09:25:22 UTC
Yesterday we did a trek to Otanewainuku Forest to look for the Happy
Humpback box, and after much hunting we found it! Thank you Judi and
Linton for hiding a box in such an amazing location. What a shame
that the weather closed in as we approached the summit, so we didn't
get to see the amazing views you talked about (and it made the track
treacherous in places - I would warn anyone contemplating this box to
make sure the track is relatively dry). We will have to go back
another time.

The landscape around the observation platform has changed a bit since
you hid the box and you might need to update your clues, but we'll
email you about that separately so it doesn't ruin the hunt for
anyone else. The box was in good condition, but might have got a few
spots of water inside due to the bad weather when we opened it,
sorry. We tried to be careful! So we didn't get to fully appreciate
the logbook as we were worried about getting things wet... : (
Another reason to go back I suppose!

Rebecca and Derek

RE: [NZLB] We found the Happy Humpback!

From: Judi Lapsley Miller | Date: 2004-09-13 21:59:19 UTC
Thank you so much for letting us know about the directions - I'll amend the
clues. We placed it in late summer and the trek wasn't too bad, but I can
imagine how boggy it must have been in places in spring. I hope you get to
go back on a fine day - it's a magical spot. If you do go back, do consider
doing a tag-along box so we have something to find next time we do a box
check :-)

Cheers,

Judi
