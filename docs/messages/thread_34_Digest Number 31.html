I'm from Christchurch & have just started letterboxing as of Sunday. A
random link I stumbled upon introduced me to the concept (I already knew
about geocaching but had never heard of letterboxing) & my daughter & I went
to hunt for the Native YEP letterbox.

Much to our surprise, it was still there 3 years after the last person had
visited it! We cleaned it up a bit, double bagged the journal (a little bit
of water had seeped into the container) & rehid it.

Now we're looking to place a few of our own round the area as there aren't
many in or near Christchurch. I guess the alternative is to carefully choose
our next holiday destination based on a few letterbox clues :)
