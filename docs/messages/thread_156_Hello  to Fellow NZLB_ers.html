From: Judi Lapsley Miller | Date: 2009-07-08 00:35:26 UTC

Welcome Heather! Sounds like you’re on the right track with your letterbox. I use the Systema containers too – they seem nicely waterproof. I tend to use the smallest ones (200ml??) as they’re easier to hide, plus I like making tiny notebooks J. I’ve never bothered with camouflage, but it could work well depending on where you’re hiding it.

 

I suggest you also get some small ziplock-style bags for the notebook and stamp (separately so as to not get ink on the other things). Put the contents in the ziplock bags, and the bags into the container. (Don’t put the container into a bag as we’ve found they tend to tear and let in water over time).

 

On the NZLB website, you’ll find in the files section a pdf and word document designed especially for printing out and putting in the box:

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/NZLB/files/Letterbox%20Info/

Feel free to add your own details to it.

 

Some people also put stamp pads in the boxes, but here in NZ they’re expensive, and the damp weather can make them run. So I no longer bother.

 

Cheers,

 

Judi (Team Psychokiwi)
