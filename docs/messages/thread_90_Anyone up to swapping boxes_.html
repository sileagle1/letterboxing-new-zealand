From: Judi Lapsley Miller | Date: 2005-02-18 22:22:50 UTC
Are you going to be in Wellington at any stage? Could be a good chance to
get some inky fingers and meet up with us (Team Psychokiwi) and Chaucey &
Michael (who we haven't met yet, despite being just down the road)!

The majority of the boxes in NZ are traveller friendly b/c I think many of
us are placing boxes when we're travelling around the country already. The
exception is some of the Wellington boxes, which are more likely to be
mystery boxes and maybe require a little research or local knowledge to
solve; some are around 1-3 hours walk up and down steep hills too so you
don't want to plan to do too many in a day. If you let us know how long
you'll be in Wellington, we can recommend which ones you'd have time to do.

How are you travelling? If you're doing the campervan thing then some of the
boxes are at camp sites, which could be handy.

Cheers,

Judi
