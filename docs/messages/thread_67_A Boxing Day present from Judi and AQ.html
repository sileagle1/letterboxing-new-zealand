Not a Christmas present, but, more appropriately, a Boxing Day present from Judi and AtlasQuest: the New Zealand trip planner! We've loaded routes for all the State Highways 1 through 8 so that you can map out a boxious route for your summer holidays.
Go to
http://atlasquest.com/lboxes/trip/
and pull down the dropdown menu to choose which highway you're travelling on.
Choose how far from the highway you're willing to detour, then choose the types of boxes you're after then hit Search. Once the search results are up you can then narrow the search to within particular Start and End waypoints along the highway.
 
Note that the trip planner does not show mystery boxes (at least ones that don't pinpoint a town), so you still may need to do a little homework. Note too that not all NZ boxes are listed on AQ - you can find a more complete, but less user-friendly list at www.psychokiwi.org/letterboxing/nzboxing.htm.
 
Wishing you all a Boxious Boxing Day on 26 Dec,
 
Cheers,
