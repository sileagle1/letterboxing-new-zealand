Yeah well the IALC gals are over-achievers :-)
I never got round to finishing my contribution to Alice - was too busy
trying to move country instead. They do use larger carving blocks - the
stuff they make the Mars erasers from can be bought in enourmous blocks. The
"pink stuff" material also comes in largish sheets. I don't know if you can
get them here, but if you're keen you could order it from Dick Blick's
online arts store. But I suggest working with erasers for a while until you
get the hang of it. Some letterbox series are based on multiple erasers -
you stamp them next to each other (or sometimes on top of each other) to
build up a larger picture.

Some of the IALC gals get the fine detail by using body-piercing needles.
I've got some but am still figuring out how to use them without impaling
myself.

Glad to see you joined IALC - we need more international members!! But have
you got your purple socks yet??
