From: Judi Lapsley Miller | Date: 2004-08-19 19:58:10 UTC
> From: Chaucey McLachlan [mailto:Chaucey@...]
> I was wondering if anyone has any special presentation tips for their
> notebooks? Do you incorporate a journal or the clue description
> etc.. near the stamp?

I'm boring in that I tend to just write the date and location and sometimes
the person/group that made the box. On occasion I've written more - for
instance if I've found a box with no clues (once you've been boxing a while
you can kind of just sniff them out - but that really only works in
box-dense areas like Connecticut and Dartmoor which have boxes under every
rock). Others keep their logbooks more like a travel journal. I've also
heard of folk who always take photos to go with the entry. Others don't keep
a logbook but stamp onto index cards. Some just stamp the clue sheet.

So it's basically a very personal thing.

Judi
