From: splendidbuccaneer | Date: 2004-10-17 20:43:00 UTC
Hi group!

I'm in Colorado (USA) and have visited the board once or twice
although I usually just lurk around in the shadows and read. :)

Would anyone here be interested in a box exchange with me? I and a
friend have both done boxes that we would love to send over to be
placed there.... and would also really enjoy receiving boxes to
plant here! Our boxes would be to do with our area... Colorado
subjects. Your boxes could be anything at all that you want. I'm
in Grand Junction and he is in Durango...we can place in both spots.

If anyone wants to play foreign exchange just shout and I'll box up
our offerings (and can send carving materials or anything else
that's easier to find here) and shoost it to you!

I'm planning on a visit NZ next year and will plant boxes then, but
I'd really like send these two first. Let me know!

Esmerelda of the Splendid Buccaneers, waving from rainy, grey
western Colorado

Thanks for the heads up on that travel book.. it was perfect! Great
advice!
