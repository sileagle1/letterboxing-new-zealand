I'm not really sure what features you most prize in a letterbox (eg cool stamp, tricky clues, lovely scenery, nice walk, proximity to other boxes etc..), so I'll just tell you some of my favourites.  Bear in mind though that I've only seen about half the boxes out there, so some of the ones I haven't seen might be AWESOME, and I just don't know it yet.

*Little Shag, Taniwha and What a fluke - Kaikoura
 
*I also liked the two boxes that Epiphany recommended in Christchurch.
