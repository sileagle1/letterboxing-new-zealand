NZ Letterboxing website updated and a general howdy

From: Judi Lapsley Miller | Date: 2009-07-04 01:54:19 UTC

Howdy – welcome to our recent new subscribers and hello to the rest of you. It’s been far too long but I finally got around to updating my list of New Zealand letterboxes, broken down by region. There has been a huge increase in boxes in the past six months. Of particular note are the dedicated efforts of Petra (jp nature) from Turangi and 55 Steps from Christchurch.

http://www.psychokiwi.org/letterboxing/nzboxing.htm

 

In other news, I heard that there was an article about letterboxing in NZ Wilderness magazine. Has anyone seen it? I couldn’t find anything on their website. Also, I was contacted by Radio NZ National last week. They’re looking for someone in the Wellington region who's a good talker and who's knowledgeable and enthusiastic about letterboxing. That isn’t me! (well the good talker bit anyway) so I passed along Chaucey’s details, but if any other Wellingtonian is interested, please get in touch as I’m not sure how active Chaucey is these days.

 

So while the weather might not be so conducive to boxing this weekend, perhaps it’s a good opportunity to plan summer holidays, do some carving, and cook up some clues. Has anyone had some recent letterboxing adventures they’d like to share with the group? We’d love to hear about them!

 

Cheers,

 

Judi (Team Psychokiwi)
