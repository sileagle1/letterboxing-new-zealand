Want to place my stamps?

From: Barbara | Date: 2005-02-19 17:50:28 UTC
Hello out there!
I've just been glancing over some messages on the board here and
noticed that a few people are willing to place stamps that others
carve. I was in NZ last December with carving materials but only
placed one box on the North Island - Kiwi Crossing - yet had hoped
to place several, especially on the South Island. I have one stamp
that I'd carved while there but never placed and had several drawn
out. I've already begun saving for my next trip out your way, but
it'll be at least 2 years most likely. In the meantime, if anyone
would like to plant my volcano stamp or my tramping stamp, let me
know and I'll mail them along with log books. I had also wanted to
make up a "Shire" stamp for Matamata, so if you'll be out that way,
let me know. By the way, you don't need to send me a stamp unless
you'd like to. I'd be happy to reciprocate if you'd like.
Oh, and while you're LBing, keep an eye out for Maneki Neko, the
good fortune cat.
BTW, NZ is incredible!

Rigelpuppy

aka Barbara Adams, Chico, CA USA

Hi Barbara - glad to hear you are already saving for the next trip :-))

I'd be happy to place some boxes for you. I'm from Matamata orginally (no I
