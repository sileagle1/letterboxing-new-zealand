We popped into the Kiwi Scraps shop yesterday - they've just moved to a new shop on the Hutt Road.
(http://www.kiwiscraps.co.nz/)
 
They do not have any "eraser" carving blocks or tools but they do have a stunning range of ink pads. They have a huge rainbow of Versa Color pads which are ideal for popping into letterboxes because they are small and cheap ($3.95 each).
 
We took the opportunity to talk to the owners and told them all about letterboxing, and they are going to consider getting in some of the carving equipment. So if you go into the shop (in person or online), do tell them you are a letterboxer. The more customers they get, the more likely they'll import the bits & pieces.
 
Judi
