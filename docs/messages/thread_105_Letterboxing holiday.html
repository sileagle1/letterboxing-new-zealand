Letterboxing holiday

From: Chaucey McLachlan | Date: 2005-07-02 09:02:50 UTC
We had a fantastic time letterboxing on our Honeymoon.

Dartmoor was wonderful and we had a lot of fun staying there and hiking
around. We weren't good at finding boxes using the usual style of British
clue, which involves lining up features with a compass. We had quite a bit
of success though with some boxes for which the clues had very precise map
grid references - then we could use our gps unit to help in the search.

The Dartmoor area is very beautiful, and we saw lots of "wild" ponies and
moorland grazing sheep. We learnt a lot about the area, the small towns,
the narrow lanes, the natural and man made rock features... We also enjoyed
very much boxing at pubs, where asking for a box is an excellent excuse to
have a pub lunch, desert or drink.

As well as boxing on Dartmoor we were also able to find a box in Edinburgh,
and one in Prague, there were also several boxes I couldn't find in Paris.
We also found Geocaches in many of the places we visited.

I'll put some photos into the yahoo folder. Hopefully I can catch up with
some of your guys, as I can't wait to "show off" my logbook :-)

Cheers,

Chaucey Ellis
