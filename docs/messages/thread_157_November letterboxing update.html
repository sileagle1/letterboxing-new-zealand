November letterboxing update

From: Judi Lapsley Miller | Date: 2009-11-08 19:14:10 UTC

Just an update on recent letterboxing activity in New Zealand.

 

We’ve just returned from a trip to Stewart Island (where we planted 2 more boxes) and Christchurch where we found a number of “55 Steps” boxes. The Canterbury region is turning into a Mecca for letterboxing, and it is well worth planning holidays to go to, or at least  pass through the region. Stewart Island, although more out of the way, is beautiful and we wish we could have stayed longer – it’s well worth the effort to visit and there are lots of boxious spots to plant new boxes.

 

Elsewhere in NZ, “jp nature” is filling up the Central Plateau and has nearly finished the alphabet series, making Tongariro another holiday destination!

 

On a personal note, we finally cracked finding 500 letterboxes – we were in the 400s when we left Connecticut, but it’s taken us six more years to get to the 500 milestone. Thank you to everyone who has placed boxes in New Zealand!

 

I’ve updated the NZ letterboxing page, so do let me know if there are any missing or any errors:

http://www.psychokiwi.org/letterboxing/nzboxing.htm

 

Have any of you also had some fun letterboxing adventures? Do you have any plans for the most important date in the letterboxing calendar? (26 December - Boxing Day of course J!).

 

Cheers,
