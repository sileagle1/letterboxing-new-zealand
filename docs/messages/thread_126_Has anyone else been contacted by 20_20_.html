From: Judi Lapsley Miller | Date: 2006-05-03 22:31:09 UTC
Hey All - yes I've definitely fallen down with my New Year's resolution to plant more boxes - one day if it ever stops raining we might get back out there!
 
Meanwhile...I've been contacted by 20/20 - they're interested in more info about letterboxing. I've replied saying I'm available by email only (Team Psychokiwi is media shy!), but if any of you are interested in 15 minutes of fame, please let me know and I'll pass along your contact details.
 
Media attention about letterboxing is always a double-edged sword. The rate of boxes going missing and being vandalized tend to increase just after a newspaper article, etc. However, especially in an area that is just starting out in letterboxing, media attention can get enough people interested to form a critical mass.
 
If you're worried about particular boxes (ones you've laboured over getting just right), I'd suggest not making the clues public. Instead make the clues "word-of-mouth", passing clue sheets around privately to those you know are genuine boxers. We've only done a few like that, but then many of ours are mystery boxes so only the motivated actually bother trying to solve them.
 
Cheers,
 
Judi
