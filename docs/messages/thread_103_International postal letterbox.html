From: Judi Lapsley Miller | Date: 2005-07-17 04:12:52 UTC
This isn't a box exchange - what will happen is that you'll receive the box
in the mail, you stamp in etc., then post it on to the next person on the
list. Rumour has it that NZ will receive the box early in its journey too.
So other than finding a post office it shouldn't take much effort for the
amount of enjoyment you'll get in return :-)

Judi
