Hi Letterboxers
I was the first to find (I think) Tipani and You should have seen my face
when I saw the stamp book. I include my geocache log
here ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Well today I went out for the FTF on Tall Tree Trek and,as this cache was
not too far away, I carried on. The only problem was I had left the printed
notes on the table at home and did not have the waypoint in the GPSr either.
I had looked at Tumonz before leaving home and decided to try and find the
cache from the mental picture, I sort of retained, in my rather porous mind.

I found the reserve OK but was accompanied through the gate by some local
muggles, so I explored the area in general, until they eventually moved on.
Then I started poking around in possible hiding places and at the second try
came up with a shiny new looking, plastic container.

Ah Ha, thinks I, and I open it up. “Wow this is a rather fancy log book ,
very flash.” Then I open it and oh gee? What the?? It turns out it is a
letterbox! And I am the first finder! Luckily I have my stamp to fulfill the
requirements for finding the letterbox.

Having replaced the letterbox I continue searching any “hide” likely to
appeal to a geocacher. After probing half a dozen spots I see a flash of
plastic and bingo!

Finding a cache sans GPSr was I thought pretty clever, but getting a bonus
“letterbox” within ten metres was certainly a bonus, and a pleasant
surprise.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Cheers
Ray
