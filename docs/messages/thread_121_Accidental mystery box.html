Accidental mystery box

From: Rebecca and Derek | Date: 2006-03-08 09:58:30 UTC
Our most recently placed letterbox is called Tipani and has been
hidden in Greenhithe. Unfortunately we misplaced the clue directions
we made when placing the box, but in my keeness to 'log' the plant I
posted it on AtlasQuest anyway, figuring that the clue could be
updated when we got the chance to check back on it.

Well, since then it seems like someone has planted a geocache within
10m of our box, and 3 geocachers have found it while searching, and
logged the find on AtlasQuest...

This is so far our most succesful box in terms of number of finds
logged, and there's not even a clue posted for it!

Very weird!
