Judi

Taniwha is part of Chuck Straub's "In Search Of" series, which is mainly
based in Connecticut. Its a series of mythical creatures like yeti, lochness
monster, etc. When we told Chuck about taniwha he insisted on giving us this
box to place when we got home.

The Taniwha is hiding along the Shoreline route of the Peninsula Walkway.
Find the (signposted) cave between Atia Point and Whaler's Bay. Take twelve
steps into the cave and look to the bottom left of the wall - there is a
natural nook covered with a rock. Hiding behind the rock is the Taniwha!
There should be enough natural light entering the cave too see what you're
doing, but if you wish to explore deeper, bring a torch (flashlight if
you're an American!).
