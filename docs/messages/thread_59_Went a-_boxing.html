Wahoo! We finally got out and about today to do some boxing and added "Murray's Last Minute Winner" to our collection. The box was partially exposed but in good condition. There was an entry from a non-letterboxer who had stumbled across it and was quite intrigued by it. We found a nice big rock and rehid the box in the same spot but with better coverage so hopefully it will not be found inadvertently again - too often they just get vandalized when found by non-boxers :-(
 
Thanks guys - and thanks for the "Where's George"  - I'd heard about the game but had not come across one before.
 
Cheers,
 
Judi
