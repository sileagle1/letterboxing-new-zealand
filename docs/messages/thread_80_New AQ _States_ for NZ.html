When using AtlasQuest for entering and searching for boxes, keep in mind that there is now two new "states" for NZ for Stewart Island and the Chatham Islands. All other islands at this stage are listed under the North or South Islands (e.g., Waiheke and Great Barrier Island) . Please note that if there is no "town" on an island, it most likely isn't in AQ - however, Ryan is happy to enter the islands as necessary. All he needs is the name of the island and its GPS coordinates.
 
Abbrevs:
North Island = NIS
South Island = SIS
Stewart Island = STI
Chatham Islands = CHI
 
Please let us know if there are any other far-flung islands that may need special consideration.
