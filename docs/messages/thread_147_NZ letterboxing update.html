From: Judi Lapsley Miller | Date: 2007-12-19 00:51:15 UTC
We're rapidly approaching the most important date in a diehard letterboxer's calendar, which is, of course, Boxing Day! We're hoping to get out and have a go at some of the new boxes that have appeared in Wellington - maybe even place some too (but no promises!). We hope you all get the chance to walk off some of that Christmas pudding and get out there boxing too.
 
With that in mind, I have updated the listing of NZ letterboxes at
http://letterboxing.psychokiwi.org/nzboxing.htm
adding in 16 recently planted boxes, including one in Antarctica - now that's extreme boxing for you! I've been rather delinquent at keeping this page up to date, but fortunately most new boxes are now listed at AtlasQuest too. Do point out if I've missed any or have made any errors.
 
Wishing you all happy, peaceful, and most of all boxious holidays,
