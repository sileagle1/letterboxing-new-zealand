Hi (hi everyone...back again!!).

I'm a bit biased towards the Chch ones :) But Roy Rogers is easy to take a
kiddie to (& you get a great view over Lyttleton Harbour...& lots of places
for kids to run round & explore)...& Native YEP is also easy to get to & a
nice walk for children.

Ralphie is a bit more of a hike (& harder to find) but if you think you can
do the walk, there's a nice cave at the end. But it is harder than the other
2.
