RE: [NZLB] Re: Join me in my New Year's resolution

From: Judi Lapsley Miller | Date: 2006-02-18 18:37:35 UTC
Good on you for also resolving to plant more! I must get my act together - I
have my February box ready to go, but just need to find the time and the
right spot to put it.

Publicity is a double-edged sword. We've been involved in magazine and
newspaper articles before, and it was a newspaper article that got us into
letterboxing in the first place. But it would seem that everytime a
newspaper article goes out, more boxes go missing or are vandalized. I
believe word-of-mouth is a better method to get more people boxing,
especially because you can take the time to fully explain the idea and to
also take them boxing. But newspaper articles can be a good way to kick
start it in an area where not much is happening.

If anyone does want to engage the media, do go ahead, and do feel free to
ask this mailing list for advice if you like. And do let us know when/where
the article will appear. Generally we want to push the idea that it's a
great activity for all the family, teaches lots of skills; also that the
"leave no trace" and respect for the environment message. Try not to mention
that some feel letterboxes could be confused with bombs - its such a red
herring (after all we don't see people get worried about litter when it too
could just as easily be a bomb!).

Cheers,

Judi
