Hello NZ!

From: Wisconsin Hiker | Date: 2007-11-23 09:02:20 UTC
Hello to the NZ letterboxers that monitor this board!

We arrived in NZ from the United States on Monday and have looked for 4
boxes so far. We found 3 of the 4 (couldn't find the Roy Rogers box on
Godley Head near Christchurch, but the clues had warned it might be
missing, so we were not too surpised to come up empty handed). We also
planted a new box near Lake Tekapo and the clues for the box are now
listed on both www.letterboxing.org and AtlasQuest.

We were surprised to discover how few people have logged into some of
the boxes. The box in Clyde was placed 3.5 years ago, but we were only
the 3rd group to stamp in!

We have 4 more boxes to place before we return home on DEC 7, and
hopefully someone will visit each of them within the next year...

Wisconsin Hiker & Martini Man
